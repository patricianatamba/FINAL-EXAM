Understanding Our Safety Ratings

Safe

Ingredients proven to be safe for general use. They nourish, protect, and heal the skin naturally.

Use with Caution

May cause irritation, dryness, or breakouts for sensitive skin. Requires proper usage or specific skin types.

Avoid

Harmful chemicals, unprescribed medical ingredients, or toxic substances that damage the skin barrier.

Why SafeSkincareUG?

With the rise of harmful skin bleaching products and unregulated cosmetics in the market, it's harder than ever to know what's safe.

We built SafeSkincareUG to provide a transparent, easy-to-understand directory of common skincare ingredients, completely localized for our Ugandan community.
