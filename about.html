Our Mission

SafeSkincareUG was founded with a singular, vital mission: to prevent skin damage from harsh, unregulated chemicals and promote informed, healthy choices in Uganda.

For too long, the local market has been flooded with products containing dangerous levels of hydroquinone, mercury, and unprescribed steroids. These ingredients promise quick fixes but result in long-term, sometimes irreversible, damage to the skin barrier and overall health.

We aim to demystify skincare labels by providing clear, accessible information in both English and Luganda, empowering consumers to choose products that truly nourish and protect their skin.

Our Vision for the Future

We envision a Uganda where every consumer can confidently pick up a skincare product, read the label, and instantly know whether it is safe for their body. We believe in celebrating natural beauty and promoting the rich, safe, local ingredients our country has to offer, from pure Shea Butter to healing Aloe Vera.

The Team Behind the Platform

We are a dedicated group of dermatological enthusiasts, local health advocates, and web developers based right here in Kampala. Combining medical research with digital accessibility, we curate our database carefully to ensure accuracy.

Every ingredient rating on this platform is verified against global dermatological standards while remaining tailored to the environmental realities of East Africa.
